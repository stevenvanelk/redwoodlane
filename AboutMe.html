About Me

Hi! I'm the founder of Redwood Lane, a dedicated Salesforce consultant specializing in Administration, Integrations, Automation, and Reporting.

With years of experience optimizing Salesforce environments, I’ve helped businesses streamline their workflows, improve data management, and automate key processes.

Before diving into the Salesforce ecosystem, I worked as a creative professional in photography and video production. Storytelling has always been at the heart of what I do, whether through visuals or digital transformation. My background in crafting compelling narratives and solving creative challenges naturally transitioned into Salesforce, where I now help organizations build scalable solutions and seamless user experiences.

Beyond Salesforce
